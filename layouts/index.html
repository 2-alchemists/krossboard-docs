Krossboard in a nutshell

Krossboard comes to help organizations owning Kubernetes clusters to tackle the challenges to make cost management and capacity planning decisions. This is achieved by introducing a global and consistent approach of resource usage analysis where the usage of all Kubernetes clusters are collected, processed, aggregated, and visualized at a single place.
